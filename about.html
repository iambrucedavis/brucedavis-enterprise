About

AI Systems Engineer. IAM and security practitioner. Forward deployed for fifteen years.

The Arc

Fifteen years across Wells Fargo, Veradigm, Starbucks, and Microsoft — payment infrastructure, HIPAA-regulated IAM, Non-Human Identity governance before NHI was a named discipline. Two threads ran underneath all of it: identity (who is allowed to do what) and audit (regulated deployments where the documentation matters as much as the code). When AI arrived, neither problem shrunk — they scaled. The career didn't pivot. It compounded. Today I build agentic AI systems with the same identity-first, audit-ready instincts.

How I Work

Forward deployed — I work embedded in customer environments, learning the domain while shipping the system. Microsoft AI Factory delivery, HIPAA-regulated healthcare IAM, Federal Reserve payment infrastructure. I don't build demos. I build systems that survive the stakeholder review, the audit, and the production incident at 2am.

On an agentic AI engagement: I design the agent architecture, build the MCP tooling, threat-model the permission surface, wire the human-in-the-loop gates, and write the evaluation harness. Then I stay through the rollout. PMP-certified — the rare AI engineer who can run the program around the build.

Full-time, contract, and project work. Remote-first.
